Topic Description

Chemical kinetics is the study of the rates of chemical reactions. It investigates how fast chemical reactions occur and the factors that influence these rates.

Rate of Reaction: The change in concentration of reactants or products per unit time.

Rate Law: An equation that expresses the relationship between the rate of a reaction and the concentrations of the reactants.

Order of Reaction: The exponent to which the concentration of a reactant is raised in the rate law.

Reaction Mechanisms: The sequence of elementary steps by which a chemical reaction proceeds.
