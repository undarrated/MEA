Topic Description

A sequence is an ordered list of numbers. These numbers can follow a specific pattern or rule.

2, 4, 6, 8, 10... (Arithmetic sequence: each number increases by 2)

1, 2, 4, 8, 16... (Geometric sequence: each number is multiplied by 2)

A series is the sum of the terms in a sequence.

For example, if the sequence is 1, 2, 3, 4, the corresponding series would be 1 + 2 + 3 + 4 = 10
