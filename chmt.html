Topic Description

Thermochemistry is the branch of chemistry that studies the heat changes associated with chemical reactions and physical transformations.

Exothermic Reactions: Reactions that release heat to the surroundings.

Endothermic Reactions: Reactions that absorb heat from the surroundings.

Enthalpy (H): A thermodynamic property that represents the heat content of a system.

Hess's Law: States that the enthalpy change of a reaction is independent of the pathway taken and depends only on the initial and final states.
